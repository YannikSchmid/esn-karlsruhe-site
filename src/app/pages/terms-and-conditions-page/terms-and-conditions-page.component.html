Article 1 – Applicability & Definitions

1.1 These terms are applicable to participants of any event organised, hosted or otherwise facilitated by ESN Karlsruhe.

1.2 In these terms, ESN Karlsruhe consists of members of the Executive Board and volunteers organising the event concerned.

1.3 In these terms, a participant is a visitor of any event organised, hosted or otherwise facilitated by ESN Karlsruhe.

1.4 In these terms, an event is every activity organised by ESN Karlsruhe for participants.

Article 2 – ESNcard

2.1 An ESNcard holder is an individual who possesses an ESNcard.

2.2 To be eligible to buy an ESNcard from ESN Karlsruhe, an individual must be:

  1. a volunteer of ESN Karlsruhe;
  2. or an international student following one or multiple courses at one of Karlsruhe’s higher education institutions;
  3. or an international student doing an internship in Karlsruhe.

2.3 An ESNcard is valid for one year after the date of purchase.

Article 3 – Ticket Sale Policy

3.1 Only one ticket per event can be bought unless stated otherwise.

3.2 Buyers of a ticket are allowed to resell their ticket to another person, under the following conditions:

  • The new owner of the ticket has to be eligible for buying a ticket (e.g.: be an ESNcard holder as defined in article 2).
  • The new owner of the ticket will only be permitted to participate in the activity if they updated their ticket details (e.g.: name, email address etc.) and informed ESN Karlsruhe about the ticket transfer at info@esn-karlsruhe.de before the activity starts.
  • ESN Karlsruhe assumes no responsibility for the transfer of tickets. Transferring tickets is at the buyer’s and seller’s own risk.

Article 4 – Event obligations

4.1 It is forbidden for participants to bring and use fireworks, (fire)arms, other weapons, dangerous objects, drugs or any other illegal objects to an event. ESN Karlsruhe will determine whether an object is dangerous or not.

Bringing alcohol is forbidden unless specifically stated otherwise.

4.2 Participants are obligated to obey the directions and/or regulations expressed by ESN Karlsruhe, its organising partners, venue staff, the security guards or any other authority. Should a participant not comply with these directions and/or regulations, or should they be guilty of endangerment, intoxication, threatening and/or violence, this will result in the automatic exclusion for the remainder of the event and possible exclusion from one or more future events.

Article 5 – Cancellation policy

5.1 Once an order has been paid, ESN Karlsruhe will not refund any tickets

5.2 ESN Karlsruhe reserves the right to reschedule an event to an earlier/later date or to cancel it

Article 6 – Liability and damage

6.1 ESN Karlsruhe will only accept liability and consequential reimbursement of any damages to the extent that is covered by ESN Karlsruhe’s insurer.

6.2 A participant will be held liable for all the damage under any circumstance for the occasion of or concerning the (build-up or break-down of an) event, caused by themselves to other visitors of the event, to employees, to possessions of ESN Karlsruhe, third parties hired by ESN Karlsruhe, to the location or any accessory of the locations of the event, or to anything else that is in the location of the event.

6.3 The participant will indemnify ESN Karlsruhe from any claims from third parties concerning the damage that was caused by the participant.

6.4 In the case of conflict between the Terms & Conditions of ESN Karlsruhe and the Terms & Conditions of a host of the event, the Terms & Conditions of the host take precedence.

Article 7 – Intellectual property & copyright

7.1 ESN Karlsruhe is permitted to take and publish photos and/or videos of an event.

7.2 Upon entering an event location, the participant and any other volunteers in service of ESN Karlsruhe give permission to publish these promotional images.

7.3 ESN Karlsruhe holds the copyright to photos and/or videos of an event that are published. All photos and or videos ESN Karlsruhe takes are the intellectual property of ESN Karlsruhe and cannot be altered without the explicit consent of ESN Karlsruhe.

7.4 ESN Karlsruhe holds the right to remove photos and/or videos of an event that are published. Photos and/or videos of an event will only be published after careful examination.

7.5 Any person depicted in the photos and/or videos has the right to request the removal of said published material by sending an email to info@esn-karlsruhe.de.

Article 8 – Privacy Policy

As a participant, you agree that the ESN Karlsruhe e.V. may use the data provided by you for organizing this event and tasks related to that. See the privacy policy of ESN Karlsruhe: Privacy Policy.
