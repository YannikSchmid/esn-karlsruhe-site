We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the Schroff Foundation for their generous support of our mission to integrate international students and foster cultural exchange.

Founded in 1984 by Ingrid and Gunther Schroff, the foundation has long been committed to education and social initiatives. Their support, including funding for student housing like the Schroff Kolleg, has enriched student life in Karlsruhe, providing affordable accommodation near the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT).

Their dedication to educational excellence and communities aligns perfectly with our values, and we are honored to recognize their invaluable contribution.
