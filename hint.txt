Do not delete this folder!

This is a redirection to the karlsruhe.esn-germany.de page with the .htaccess file.

Content of the htaccess file:

# Make HTTPS the default
<IfModule mod_rewrite.c>
RewriteEngine On

# force to esn-germany page | odi
#RewriteCond %{HTTP_HOST} ^esn-karlsruhe.de$ [NC]
RewriteRule ^(.*)$ https://karlsruhe.esn-germany.de/$1 [R=301,L]

RewriteCond %{HTTPS} !=on
RewriteCond %{ENV:HTTPS} !=on
RewriteRule .* https://%{SERVER_NAME}%{REQUEST_URI} [R=301,L]
</IfModule>
